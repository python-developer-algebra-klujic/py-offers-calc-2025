Customer Types Description

The Customer Types model in the Django web application for "Mala firma j.d.o.o." distinguishes between two types of customers: Company and Person.

A Company represents corporate clients and includes details such as the company name, address, contact person, email, phone number, and VAT number.

On the other hand, a Person represents individual clients and includes details such as full name, address, email, and phone number.

This categorization helps in managing customer data more effectively and tailoring offers accurately based on whether the customer is a corporate entity or an individual.

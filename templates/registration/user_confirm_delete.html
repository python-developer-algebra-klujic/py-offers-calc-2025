Users Description

The User Roles model in the Django web application defines four distinct roles: Administrators, CEO, Managers, and Employees.

  • Administrators have full access to all features and settings, allowing them to manage the entire system.
  • CEO has high-level access to view and analyze overall business performance and strategic data.
  • Managers oversee specific departments or projects, with permissions to manage teams and resources within their scope.
  • Employees have access to perform their job-specific tasks and manage daily operations.
This role-based system ensures that users have appropriate access and permissions according to their responsibilities, enhancing security and efficiency.
